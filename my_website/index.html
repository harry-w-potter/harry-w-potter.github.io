Introduction to websites!

Definition of a website

A website is a set of related web pages located under a single domain name, typically produced by a single person or organization. ——From Oxford Languages

Uses of a personal website

  1. Job Applications
    • With finding work harder than ever, having a personal website can make a huge leg up, especially when most people don't. All you need to do is to show your website to the employer and you can make a very good first impression on them.
  2. Networking
    • You can link your social media account to your website, this way, you can let more people get to know you and have more friends. Imagine trying to find a friend on social media, it's like trying to find a needle in the ocean, but this way os connecting to the web allows other people to find you precisely and efficiently.
  3. Traveling
    • You could put a section of your website that is dedicated to keep track of what you've done and where you've been. This way of memory wall can be shown to everybody and allow your friends to keep up to date of what you're doing.
  4. Blogging
    • Blogging is always a great way of showing how creative and interesting you can be, also advertising your writing skill. This could come in handy when applying for a job and also to let more people know about you and your ideas.
  5. Creativity Expression and Web Design
    • Your personal website is a accomplishment of itself, demonstrating the skill you have at web designing and programming. It lets other people have a fresh view on you and your skills, and also learn more about you on your website.
  6. Your external hard drive
    • When I'm doing school homework, programming and doing other things, my computer gets really messy and I usually need to dend file between my desktop, laptop, and ipad. But with a website, you could just put your files there and get them whenever you want as long as you have WI-FI.
